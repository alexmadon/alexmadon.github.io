--- layout: post title: avconv audio convert date: '2013-07-23T03:16:00.001-07:00' author: Alex Madon tags: unix modified_time: '2013-07-23T03:16:50.388-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7521237649117347170.post-3254756228737675168 blogger_orig_url: http://tuxunix.blogspot.com/2013/07/avconv-audio-convert.html --- avconv -i INPUT -map 0 -c:v libx264 -c:a copy OUTPUT

avconv -i the_croods.mp4 -map 0 -c:v copy -c:a libmp3lame the_croods_mp3.mp4

to have audio and video in sync:

avconv -i the_croods.mp4 -map 0 -c:v copy -c:a libmp3lame -async 1 the_croods_mp3.mp4
