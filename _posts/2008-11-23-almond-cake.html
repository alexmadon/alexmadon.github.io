--- layout: post title: almond cake date: '2008-11-23T02:41:00.000-08:00' author: atpic tags: cooking modified_time: '2008-11-23T02:49:17.431-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4344287556980631462.post-5355549882077903084 blogger_orig_url: http://tuxcook.blogspot.com/2008/11/almond-cake.html --- Ingredients 225g butter 225g sugar 3 large eggs 125 flour + 1 tea spoon raising agent (sodium bicarb.) 200g ground almonds cream the butter and sugar together with a spoon or your hands beat the eggs add the eggs to the butter and suger mixture mix the flour, raising agent and ground almonds add the flour and almond mixture to the eggs sugar and butter mixture put in a oven dish and cook around 40 minutes at 150 C decorate with sliced almonds and icing sugar
