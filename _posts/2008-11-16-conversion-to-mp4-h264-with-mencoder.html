--- layout: post title: conversion to mp4 h264 with mencoder date: '2008-11-16T02:56:00.001-08:00' author: atpic tags: cooking modified_time: '2008-11-16T02:56:51.153-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4344287556980631462.post-5314315076287820316 blogger_orig_url: http://tuxcook.blogspot.com/2008/11/conversion-to-mp4-h264-with-mencoder.html --- mplayer -ao pcm -vc null -vo null MVI_6176.AVI # This produces a file called audiodump.wav faac --mpeg-vers 4 audiodump.wav mencoder MVI_6176.AVI -vf scale=400,harddup -ovc x264 -x264encopts bitrate=1000:threads=auto:frameref=5:bframes=0:nob_pyramid:nob_adapt:direct_pred=auto:subq=6:mixed_refs:nodct_decimate:no_psnr -nosound -noskip -of rawvideo -o output.264 MP4Box -add output.264 -add audiodump.aac output.mp4
