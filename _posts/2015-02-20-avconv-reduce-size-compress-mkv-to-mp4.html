avconv -i book_latino.mkv -vcodec libx264 -map 0:0 -acodec libmp3lame -map 0:2 book_latino_small.mp4

mkv to mkv reducing size and keeping subtitles:



avconv -i ice_age2.mkv -vcodec libx264 -map 0:0 -acodec libmp3lame -map 0:1 -acodec libmp3lame -map 0:2 -scodec copy -map 0:3 -map 0:4 -map 0:5 -map 0:6 -map 0:7 ice_age2b.mkv

forcing bitrate of 512k
avconv -i ice_age2.mkv  -vcodec libx264 -b 512k -map 0:0 -acodec libmp3lame -map 0:1 -acodec libmp3lame -map 0:2 -scodec copy -map 0:3 -map 0:4 -map 0:5 -map 0:6 -map 0:7  ice_age2b.mkv
