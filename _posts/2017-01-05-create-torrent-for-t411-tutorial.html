Creation du .torrent



http://askubuntu.com/questions/32024/how-to-create-a-torrent-using-the-command-line

Find you passkey in your t411 user profile


http://www.t411.li/users/profile/
Niveau de Confidentialité: Normal
Votre Clé: e17xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx (passkey)


This will give you the URL of the announce server:


http://t411.download/e17xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx/announce


transmission-create \
-o mon_film.torrent \
-c "Mon Film" \
--private \
-t http://t411.download/e17xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx/announce \
mon_film.mkv


Conclude


upload in t411 your torren, you nfo, your bbcode description
